# KaS

KaS is a personal finance app for tracking money in one place.

KaS mobile is coming soon to the App Store and Google Play.

It offers:
- transaction tracking
- monthly budgets
- account balance tracking
- credit card debt tracking
- subscriptions and bills management
- recurring income tracking
- savings goals
- charts and financial summaries
- statement import support
- an in-app AI assistant for quick finance actions and questions
